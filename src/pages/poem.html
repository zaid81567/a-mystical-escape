A Strange Memory

There's a strange memory, a strange story!
Where it starts and where it ends?
Where does this destiny take us?
Neither they understood nor I.

Why does this smoke form when lights are switched on?
I dreamed of this, when I myself stay in delusions.

Congrats! That you are someone's pride,
Being so close to someone, that went far away from everyone.
Carrying loads of love from everyone and settling into a new world.

Whenever this evening will arrive, it will carry your thoughts the same way!

A strange memory, a strange story.

~Samiha
